## Mergers and Hexquisitions

![Mergers and Hexquisitions](screenshots/screenshot000.png "Mergers and Hexquisitions")

### Description

You are the only hope left for your company to survive, use magic hexes to manipulate the market! Reduce the value of companies you want to buy by magically committing tax evasion on their behalf or causing unexpected disasters.

### Features

 - Buy or Sell companies.
 - Use magic to manipulate the market and change the value of the companies.

### Controls

 - Drag to browse available companies.
 - Hover over runes on the right to see available spells.
 - Click runes to use them.
 - Use on screen buttons to but or sell companies.
